Changing Sounds in Simple Words

New words can be formed by changing one sound in the beginning, middle, or end. Changing a sound gives the word a different meaning.

Examples:

• Change the beginning sound: cat → hat

• Change the middle sound: cat → cot

• Change the ending sound: cat → cap

Playing with sounds helps you read and spell new words. It also makes you notice the sounds in words and how they work together.
