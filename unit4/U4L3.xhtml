Inferring the Character’s Feelings

Have you ever seen someone who was smiling? What do you think that person was feeling? How did you know that? You looked at their face, right? That is called using clues.

In a story, the author does not always tell us how the character feels. But we can use clues, like what the character says, does, and how he or she looks like, to figure it out. That skill is called inferring.

GRAMMAR

Using Words That Describe

Describing words tell more about a person, place, animal, or thing. They give details about how something looks, feels, smells, sounds, or tastes.

Describing words help make sentences and stories more interesting and easier to imagine.

Examples:

  • The red apple (looks)
  • The soft pillow (feels)
  • The loud music (sounds)
  • The sweet candy (tastes)
  • The fragrant flower (smells)

Describing words help readers see, hear, or feel what is happening in the story.

Using describing words also makes writing more fun because it adds more details.

Listen as your teacher reads the myth about where the first plant came from.

A myth is a make believe story to explain where things come from or how things came to be. Myths usually have magical creatures or elements.

Lito and the First Plant
Amy R. Pomoy

Long ago, there were no plants on Earth. The ground was dry and bare.

In a small barangay, there lived a kind boy named Lito. He loved helping his mother fetch water and sweep the yard. One day, Lito saw a shiny green stone near the river. It was glowing softly.

When he picked it up, he heard a whisper say, “Bury me in the ground and water me everyday.”

Lito was surprised but he did what the voice said. He dug a small hole, placed the green stone inside, and covered it with soil.

Every day, he visited the spot. He gave it water and sang a little song: “I don’t know what you are, my friend. But I hope you bring new life that will never end.”

Days passed, and something amazing happened—a small green sprout appeared from where the green stone was buried! It grew and grew until it became a tall tree with shiny leaves.

His neighbors were amazed. The tree gave shade, fruits, and cool air.

From that day on, people started planting the seeds from the tree all around. They said the tree came from Lito’s care and kindness. That’s how plants began to grow on Earth.
