Combining Sounds to Form Words

Listen as your teacher reads the following words.

cat
vet
cop
bus
map

Words are made up of letters. Each letter has a corresponding sound. When you combine the sounds of the letters, you can form words.

Example: When the sounds of the letters /c/, /a/, and /t/ are combined, the word cat is formed.

Say the name of each picture and write the missing letter in the box. Then write the whole word on the blank.
