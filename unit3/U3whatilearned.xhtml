Help the Characters

Read the short story.

One rainy night, a strong storm hit Luis’s town. The wind blew hard, and the heavy rain flooded the streets. Luis saw his neighbors struggling to carry their bags to a safer place. He quickly called his father, and together, they helped the neighbors move to a dry shelter.

Do the following.

  1. Find and circle the action words in the story.
  2. What problem did Luis’s community face? Draw it and write one sentence to explain the problem.
