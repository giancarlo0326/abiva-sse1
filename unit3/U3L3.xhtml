Guess Who?
Thelma Grace Abiva-Leung

They clean the rooms and make them bright,
They sweep the floors both day and night.
They guard the gates to keep us safe,
And greet us all with a smiling face.
They help the sick and make us strong,
With gentle hands the whole day long.
They work with love in all they do,
They are invisible heroes—can you guess who?
