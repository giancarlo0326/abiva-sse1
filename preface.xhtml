The Soaring for Success in English 1 worktext will take you on an adventure filled with exciting stories and fun activities. As you read the stories, we hope that you will remember your own experiences and feelings. We also hope that you will discover new adventures as you learn with us.

This book was made following the standards in the Revised K to 10 Curriculum of the Department of Education. We picked the lessons to make sure you learn all the important things you need to know for your grade!

This worktext has four units. The lessons begin with topics about the self and gradually move toward bigger ideas. Unit one, All About Me, focuses on you and your personal experiences. Unit two, To School We Go, includes selections about your school, your friends, and your school activities. Unit three, Community Life, takes you beyond your home as you explore the larger community. Lastly, unit four, One Country, One World, focuses on your country and the environment.

Each lesson begins with a picture and a list of words. By the time you finish the lesson, you will know more about those words. Every lesson has a section that takes you on an exciting trip to learn new things:

Starting Post. This is where your journey begins and where you prepare for the lesson. An activity is given to help you discover what you already know about the topic. You will also learn new ideas in every lesson.

Launching Pad. This section focuses on listening and speaking. You will listen to sounds, poems, stories, songs, and rhymes. You will learn to listen carefully and attentively while having fun.

You will also enjoy conversations with your classmates. You will talk about yourself, your family, your school, and your community. In this section, you will always find interesting topics to discuss.

Blasting Off. This is where you get to read and enjoy a new story. It will help you learn new words. It will teach you to guess what might happen next in the story. It will also test how much you understand the story.

Getting Ahead. This section is all about grammar. The lesson begins with a short activity or dialogue. It is followed by exercises that help you practice and build your language skills better.

Recording Your Journey. In this section, you will write about your thoughts, ideas, and experiences. You will practice your writing and spelling skills as you express yourself.

Touchdown. In this section, you will practice viewing. Pictures, photos, drawings, illustrations, or sometimes cartoons and comic strips will help you understand the lesson more. These pictures will also help you reflect on the values presented in the lesson.

Applying What You Learned. At the end of each unit, you will apply what you learned through a performance task This task will help show how well you understood the lessons. More importantly, it gives you the chance to apply everything you have learned.


We hope that as you go through the Soaring for Success in English Grade 1 worktext, your wings will spread wide and help you soar high on your journey to learning English skills. May this book help you grow not only in the mind but also as a person who spreads kindness and love to others. Never stop spreading your wings, flying high, and aiming for success.
