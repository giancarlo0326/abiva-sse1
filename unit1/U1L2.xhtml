Listen as your teacher says the following words from the song.

if it happy fun clap

Do you hear the sound at the start of each word? The first sound in a word is the beginning sound. Try making the beginning sound of each word with your teacher.

Now, listen as your teacher says the following words.

clap stomp feet

Do you hear the sound at the end of each word? The ending sound is the last sound you hear when you say a word. This is also the letter name of the sound.
