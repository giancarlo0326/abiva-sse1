READING

Getting Ready

Peter likes playing with his cousin, Jayjay. One sunny day, they met at the park. Read the story with your teacher and find out what fun adventures they had.

Peter and Jayjay
Thelma Grace Abiva-Leung

Mama and Peter were excited to go to the park. It was a sunny day. Peter wanted to play on the swing.

At the park, Peter saw his cousin, Jayjay.

What do you think Peter and Jayjay will do at the park?

They played hide-and-seek behind the trees. Jayjay was really good at hiding!

After that, they slid down the shiny slide together. They also played on the swing as many times as they could. When Jayjay saw a dragonfly, he called Peter. They ran after it flew away.

After a while, they became hungry, so they asked their moms for some snacks. Mama bought them yummy sandwiches!

After playing, they said goodbye to Jayjay and his mom. Peter went home tired but happy. He told Mama he had a lot of fun playing with Jayjay. Mama smiled and tucked Peter in bed. It is time to sleep and rest.


UNDERSTANDING

Answer the following questions about the story:

  1. Why were Peter and Mama excited to go to the park?

  2. Who did Peter see at the park?

  3. How did Peter feel after playing in the park?
